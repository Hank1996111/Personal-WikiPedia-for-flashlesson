**The Rundown:** OpenAI just **[announced](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.eCbm_1zon7G0lMoXTECWa-IUY9yqSc2cx0km5OJXo-MOqkgQRleiUndn1XRuFghWzBCMoDas_1Nh_Z6z7YNQwnRM_OiGUkAz8Yx9mLdbJk_JXfDSedFd03aOp8tsukKrCNoGJhVw3sw5YlEsaA6fV1PUn9xLNJsXu8V0z3pe8fPeM3uikJt9LS9v3kvbuUSfNjPMQNToEzdMxT52NzNJsXDgkJkV0cjDgX6caMDSpw-ldHgA6JT3xmzInk_6Cl9AvhwIJ-0zmddyS9pmptIRr0A1lkhCmWONQsddD0ruUzNKIQFK-O3J5w5QNjsRl_r33CnL0EzIl8-LcfJsLmvdKQ/4jl/OpVro85BRpajBuq9DxjDQg/h12/h001.ksmJ1MLPGVdEjooxSqDwZAQMIfa_Lmz9EqQDTV1SMyQ)** a $1.1B all-stock acquisition of experimentation platform Statsig, bringing its founder Vijaye Raji aboard as CTO of Applications to lead ChatGPT and Codex engineering.


**The details:**


- Raji will oversee product engineering across ChatGPT and Codex, reporting to Applications CEO Fidji Simo, who recently joined from Instacart.

- Statsig's platform, which powers A/B testing and feature flagging for companies including OpenAI itself, will continue operating independently.

- The deal marks OpenAI's second major acquisition attempt this year, following a **[failed](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.dSnm3kaGd0BkNqLYPjeMf75St_4h4uVDlb2B1USeB_EJPVrRXdK94YW335JUSkPmbLYTDblntUMpjVYZLa1vQYBJ3QAy8VN_icRZCYB9NcTmlXYLzhW2aQQ3D0GDUjPXLyEPHaUpM4oQ3lAzq0qTx8P5CP8n7jcSz8h5U_O24UlLYIAuASOMWn8arc98qiHzJYOtVQzOyyGLsBtaS13WIW4_dYoc0R1HBkTjLnVXuWfg9gMTgnW5Rejz5ho_IkQBG9KuDEFOOEMlDyDQDy5ia4d9VQqkHLO69miaMhWDZ_xpJm8rli8x8lqlwnbKKOZ6_VT-utYk0nY9pqXfudlrnrIY_LRaEcbsOgqFSlnLULhZWtilSmKL1oUwHwLbejV1DcNIhq2iEoY-nE3fEzg5qyea_rbpLR2CfC_9tuQioxGZ1dC3iQsdarg-u6dUEQmFZSWWdZMP5FJu-5lIQWHo8bc4x_iYdL1JYepFlnXmuQxCl-DDDI1xK1fLaYEMNH1nwoL6GOv9nb-idaX__BmkRlqzfQn9yGrbTHkHOHiWjcCliiyzjgileaaRTb9ZzSgkKYJAKZUWssazgzaTj8-9t7BUATa4gWg3hLQAXQy_bevg-KtB0jaDvwxI-LmoTaO7JCLyH-Eb-bm71jOCn48V4lc9CSf-M0m4V7DkzlkUM1wPpSk6Cz_BvJHOo9kQBDG79vL5ZZyXeEPbQsslflQROeDCSB_6h-\_ZphRQ8_DPwDW0r_G4Wq8BaB9ssEaXb19Jsbd2iFHNpGnZNVM9DPg3yE4SuCPZ11zoCWUF3obr2mo/4jl/OpVro85BRpajBuq9DxjDQg/h13/h001.M5PZiNZCFD_h4cSr7ys-5aafih42iiCgO2aveH2PMbs)** $3B bid for coding startup Windsurf in July.

- OAI also made several other executive moves, with Srinivas Narayanan shifting to CTO of B2B Apps and CPO Kevin Weil leading a new “AI for Science” team.


**Why it matters:** OpenAI’s last acquisition was a bumpy ride, but the leadership movement appears to have this one on solid ground. With the competition for talent at an all-time high in the AI race, the acqui-hire route may become an even more utilized tool for the giants of the space to bring in top leaders and teams in areas of need.
