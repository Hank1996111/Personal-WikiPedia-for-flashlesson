**The details:**


- Norwood debuted in a comedy sketch last month, with Xicoia developing unique backstories, voices, and narrative arcs for the character.

- Xicoia is a spin-out of production studio Particle6, with founder Eline Van der Velden wanting Tilly to “be the next Scarlett Johansson or Natalie Portman”.

- Van der Velden said studios went from dismissing AI to actively pursuing deals in just months, claiming that “the age of synthetic actors isn’t coming, it’s here.”

- Several actors **[spoke out](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.s9F2vg9H0NMFC01qj9PgtIbmlz21JRE9l1tU1qzUfw-1hQN2EJkDQiDnNKumfonb1UUcLo598WVXhzokSMKzLJ1xDQYj_uDfVXLV4N5OS2KDMM_gySLphg-kzvKYdLdPvCKeZMeFYR7zTEXwup6gS2oHC58tewqF4E0WCMVO3_PNzdR94-YTuMdjgYKd8QbjzQSot-ZdDcSoy3HoKGbFUhdBZaVs7B8Adb5t0X7Tv38v93764wHyefTpYKnChTfjf9wZ4ZPZUS9hwix7gMg-MSXK9MNc_6IrlqOJJDR2CCqtHVZPn8c8Az_l5Xbcfu_ePJUAv31dJUzvrhI7NqJ4rQ/4kb/492H0toiQqOfZBIWJCVUSA/h8/h001.Mh8TKR0HUScyuDi3Cz4dDcSVNN1kPe2P8GvD099agd0)** against the potential talent deal, calling for other performers to drop the agency that signs Norwood.
