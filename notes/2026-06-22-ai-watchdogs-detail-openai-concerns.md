**The Rundown:** Two AI watchdogs **[introduced](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.dSnm3kaGd0BkNqLYPjeMf8iEQUPyYZLy3l9_bF3nUKdKXCmiVkIzV3j9aw0hedD5fOYabcS6ZlyF2mQWaWxLSC9zR-7HG4Pbm9uW7T1nE5FWHQe-kggxnuq9Qjzo7fhBOPRMQ6Uun9BX4kY7__55LsJtSlkTuWKfx429r3Kvut-T-sL8p1TeiXLVZYkomZfTQAiMZ9ofrJ4N5GtAakrqHyZlwfdLHU7Ar25Yw5RTrBJQciz6f--F8vN6fxMLHBBG/4hh/myBJzjXtRDuQ-ef4E-oB-Q/h11/h001.\_N9D9eAE8mNr9htna-Zq5Rx4T0csW0vUJbwbBsRlVE8)** the OpenAI Files, a hub of documents, testimonies, and information identifying potential conflicts of interest, mapping the organizational structure, and bringing transparency to governance failures at OpenAI.


**The details:**


- The Midas Project and the Tech Oversight Project created the collection, archiving and providing analysis on public information and testimonies.

- The report details findings in four major areas: Restructuring, CEO Integrity, Transparency & Safety, and Conflicts of Interest.

- The Files also aim to **[map](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.dSnm3kaGd0BkNqLYPjeMf8iEQUPyYZLy3l9_bF3nUKcim7kyiSI8UynvJV8H1GSh4dpjscy72Uw_hMTpPa0F0_XPnqjlpgV0QOeXYfgeKT24S2VoUH-fws5yY89-\_KS0rx5mcfQoqAEc1ZMpC7gFEob2SiU3OHXisOdSuHti2PVe5UFqHLP7wsj3qQnvipG4w-ParAKzZiLfsSy4sDPhqQVVriF7L3Oka837VZ3G9ojGN7goeOkl68rD2HdfrT0iz_V_n2h6t7D7ScV40FLWxA/4hh/myBJzjXtRDuQ-ef4E-oB-Q/h12/h001.tQOqo9U2NqNyz-r9p5VBLbUkdoGcfDHbw1WNs5WF-J8)** OpenAI’s convoluted business structure, raising concerns about the details surrounding the company’s transition to a PBC.

- The initiative also **[published](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.dSnm3kaGd0BkNqLYPjeMf8iEQUPyYZLy3l9_bF3nUKcwaHttrheD9Opg4uciz8Slz0yEvarmRM1l2eA7kZsnmNJJSuYBZTrpXjZ_UDIG5LTNNJeNRQGKZ5PbhgebFTFP6lOaqqTFP22h23A3j9miYlCAGyqeq4MFExKyfxqDKAfPFI1k1blsHsvb7IZV_C6JBmkojTPAv33-rNJjLfci5L3563pWXFRg4nvziL4YXNoW7TxvN50ICCXP5zTS5gzwo3EPYzuA7-16EkCjUOui4A/4hh/myBJzjXtRDuQ-ef4E-oB-Q/h13/h001.0xLWysLzBNqEI_ImcLWXeTLf5TbRNkp-lQxyvQrnDF4)** a “Vision for Change,” proposing a plan for OpenAI to meet the “exceptionally high standards” AI firms must be held to.


**Why it matters:** There has been no shortage of documents and reporting throughout OpenAI’s evolution from small lab to the most polarizing AI company in the world, and this latest drop brings the controversies and critiques front and center. As we inch closer to AGI, transparency on those who hold the keys is more important than ever.
