**The details:**


- The system captures subtle facial expressions, upper body movements, hands, and backgrounds from a single driving performance video.

- Requiring just a single character reference photo, Act-Two animates and maps the driving video while maintaining backgrounds and art styles.

- Runway claims the model delivers major performance gains over October 2024’s Act-One **[release](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.NN3LcSSNlElMIHwAnhfMXQrjuKMaJC_7Mj1w-cqaosnbCrJGW0Qb-4PXJxmZ9yI6awJ2eZg1X9l0JqoefxCC71E4aBCHdbQrZJquZlbTgEhM7gR1AYMCwfhM8AUC5sQhZRHUOQbGjOlawt8sDMridcqJENi3aWCzZsp_IllEugFDpdI8r6Tg876-4ffc9AfA72xKa7GAnT8lbLk9_FmCt_Pru3_rZmWSnRPHMls2FzyhdtYE7V0qQkmd6J0QPch540scuhYI2kYtCtfTMwZZIFMzRl3g9bKXDr4aP5fQGKA/4i8/fe_pxowTS6Ok-AlFXG7sGQ/h15/h001.oSW4JvtubJ6D_G5wavGdNHvwnhRju-3kwJ6bUbMksXk)**, particularly in consistency, fidelity, and movement.
