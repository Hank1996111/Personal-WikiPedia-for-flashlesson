**The Rundown:** MIT researchers just **[developed](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.ptzCNad3P3I6BL6vWVKYhBK-lMXKF9KhCoHG-fRuFMo6IoUcJ5jE2L4udtIwIBgkHOu46ip0NPh4MvsX5hBMmZZRCbqPiShWEYQvszz72xF2u65YhtpwKmLVL_fHrKunsFiUIKn7lP02UM-mWc_Ai75mBSu4CF7JWiczYE8SW1Bq1dym8NI1jKhpq3eSf1vVWK0cAcwHPWe8konG16tQYfDmqRvVZjaRwtpE7zuWC48frm8m-h-R9gm5pCqBlt85/4he/AEO51RrnTuOedwwC4hJDQg/h6/h001.Bx_PgtlOHaS4IygdMdGbgPpVgED7Eu6HzWd-oCFc6Xo)** Self-Adapting LLMs (SEAL), a framework that enables large language models to teach and improve on their own by creating their training data and instructions for self-updates.


**The details:**


- SEAL allows models to generate their own "self-edits" — instructions for creating synthetic data and setting parameters to update their own weights.

- It learns through trial-and-error via a reinforcement learning loop, rewarding the model for generating self-edits that lead to better performance.

- In knowledge tasks, the AI learned more effectively from its own notes than from learning materials generated by the much larger GPT-4.1.

- The system also dramatically improved at puzzle-solving tasks, jumping from 0% with standard methods to 72.5% after learning how to train itself effectively.


**Why it matters:** Self-improving AI is frequently mentioned as a potential lead-in to the leap toward superintelligence. While SEAL (and other research frameworks like Sakana’s **[DGM](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.dSnm3kaGd0BkNqLYPjeMf75St_4h4uVDlb2B1USeB_E3ghHBRI1pkcUBXB_TuwQgRxSXBN_RpeJYiF4MzDhDIpHiYPg-XF2Y3E3lCiR4nhTBJJNhwS4cTGlCWFm7Rj0k7RqOKpC4LAE9QIXTu2KFRUuFg2jNSqczhBu1OErVhdPkQrCXRMMeF-jzB9rJXWJm7gUdrGIm-UqGBaxEzl6pdM__N5My5OLWzz5mxfuIhH66Y2KV_JOEm_uGE58HPghYzDaTU1RwWPxtGXBauOt1uEobYdhKkLDSA8zlH-VVCjj1X9gDS_g8dwDF6W3C6fKYQpgXi_QjE34BaIcSf4BCkBL2VnlWoUQq2aQxEMf_zxwLZV4SGTPE7-x_XshgwX31/4he/AEO51RrnTuOedwwC4hJDQg/h7/h001.tJhVY1zdKcgDCeZIu5S40vNNE0ZIYPrKsrWGKcQcoCs)**) aren’t there yet, they point to a scary but exciting future where models can continue upgrading (exponentially) on their own, going beyond human design.
