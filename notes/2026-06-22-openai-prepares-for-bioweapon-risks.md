**The details:**


- OpenAI **[anticipates](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.WqXVGszJN1JEIu4aat7tRYKwhBs4HopawaECOO5F-SWOZTydY66bFdfzgvU4ahv8R7CmO9N_DMucTVEJNJuf-xVqaPnSCHM8CuhWVWjzrPm2xieuPrPCSEtdwMWR-V_pVwPifxYgEob6XCwv1vYR9SwgbB2e2ogCazTGrW3gji5IYEAJ1TdIHvDZw0S1dQ6chvgaBwQpkRiUuew5isZrvGtf6aJ9DHEL0Y7LjBAoJEDJoQWGboQvC5SmI-V5s7yKhqFctbhC8hCHQkajVR5DkfRC7m9JUC6ccxbteIhUiLw9Rr5otDpgwRoGAYtQoYDZ5XXX8HRsKcCxb_IrHpkaTQ/4hi/B4k70vZzTemkPeNueoBjPA/h15/h001.8HcRf6pn3XuSnUStMheAiTPJK8ATTXjdWnLfEptCWI8)** successors to its o3 reasoning model will trigger the “high risk” status under its **[preparedness framework](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.a3gBHu6_kDRL6l3yEfNWAQEHmE4ZhsXPCMfIhFjtNJ_yXgXD_IAoo7WI2zMlLAcmXso5Eebv3EC81CcV1TqmP-N4Pd4_bEppP7iYqjF17xCnh1rcQpTe31DOaf0ppyu-MY_9_5my7iLAiJs7JJiMcNVGMfTzbfM6SZaZ3bUfNg7ky5GrWcnPO0av9OqoCh4z6bJT9RygC6BnxYUgGbe7E1JgP2Rg5HmRCev1LtZ8OLPlW4ygWX7B-bkoxCwcu0H09iYl7feIag1rlQvga0uijuGzQ4TqVpWJme3NhrctIDGPWf7wMOQMGG8_IYuoZjIds8HTjVA5iDL-hrWw0phEGQ/4hi/B4k70vZzTemkPeNueoBjPA/h16/h001.ioit-jXr-t-lFZw75hUn6R6ofFQkoddE_VfBZMop-EE)** for biological threats.

- Mitigations include training models to refuse harmful requests, deploying always-on systems to detect suspicious activity, and advanced red-teaming.

- The company is also planning a July biodefense summit with government researchers and NGOs to discuss risks, countermeasures, and research.

- The move follows similar safety measures from Anthropic, which recently **[activated](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.dSnm3kaGd0BkNqLYPjeMf937MUSrYzK6JzB2n81ON3zt4U4jEWcK1UFUPwYUZlq39jQQH-GliAFz5lXFT0jL1ZnrufqmBhJEVueBOGj9w_Ow7RUY3fWJDVeEYLpCjY_LqzEgxPk6UE5to4VD7yPEdqELw4Bao8Q77ZocEDFYS0WyssaO3J2nSm3LdvUwletQLKR1YmIOsGf5zW6tdH_7yjvNqeiiwmmIvlOa1CaTtqYK6CMh_qKNTJOnoW55b-I4X-whuVIyg99S7TAUD_nkwdBHbIwGfUvt6h9NZPyfWGs/4hi/B4k70vZzTemkPeNueoBjPA/h17/h001.CpYG4hMFhWh_u3-CGmjHSn1iuH1unF43gizDz9n9wP8)** stricter protocols for its Claude 4 family release.


**Why it matters:** The same capabilities that could unlock scientific breakthroughs will also enable some extremely dangerous stuff in the wrong hands — and this next step up in models will take the stakes to a serious new level. While the additional safeguards and proactive moves are positive, we’re quickly about to enter very unknown territory.
