**The Rundown:** OpenAI just **[announced](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.eCbm_1zon7G0lMoXTECWa-IUY9yqSc2cx0km5OJXo-M_6kseGchxPN6_T6H3iFSamUxumwm6HxEPHmXBGKrVZC-MFbAdOgLPMN3ex5rv9wCSM2AEso9B83lcfO2x_Xn1ufYIwIvIfZM66GpRbrU6WFPVSYwdQRyFjM8BL6Q69YMmokEOQkltnUApN7APHBqq3QrWCbIFals40YvIwEc159tO7Knrjwq_oLJSihqyNucUIp3Ll8CCCGrgzUbhGijdk3tbXegrjFom4txfRE9q8B3RcoqyP56iF4MmLO0X-ZA/4io/YKF-aS0eSN2YdZdi2fADxQ/h12/h001.XSZVaGS6i4zfo6oKlrAICbxFVY3GBisd7eoxqRp5FUQ)** the launch of Stargate Norway, the company’s first European AI data center that will house 100,000 Nvidia GPUs and run entirely on renewable energy by late 2026.


**The details:**


- The facility near Narvik will start with 230MW of capacity, expandable to 520MW, making it one of Europe's largest AI computing centers.

- The project leverages Norway's cool climate and renewable energy grid, with waste heat from GPUs being redirected to power local businesses.

- Norwegian industrial giant Aker and infrastructure firm Nscale committed $1B for the initial phase, splitting ownership 50/50.

- Norway also becomes the first European partner in the “OpenAI for Countries” program, **[introduced](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/u001.eCbm_1zon7G0lMoXTECWa7ORuBtf3F3YVaN1MFvQ4xMluUzguUWoNzDVtFSaTRyBDXspYFIELNHmXNTY3HXLI_Xg2SRcy1bwn91YcbaYP79OYEYGtQgdAAmRKq1ebIuWMmGCgDmW3zjxCMwkSEiEXaCixJAk_b03fxGP4H1EnPM6hz7iyNOELNGGa-fu688sEDIMCcQyhSBtzSEXOHeCRWIbR73vVhdCVKF48uil4ooHziLRrMGEuY4jNBIWHPGkvr_zFbo4ZJIRZNj7fSX83T-n7yNoa2S-1LWXRHnTZi0/4io/YKF-aS0eSN2YdZdi2fADxQ/h13/h001.Xto4t2WtCI_7v5cqdxisvAQapg4aR14LEgRgqgd7230)** in May.
